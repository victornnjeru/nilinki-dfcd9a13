

## Nilinki - Live Band Booking Platform (MVP)

A platform connecting event planners with live bands worldwide, featuring band showcases, reviews, and event discovery.

---

### üè† **Public Pages**

**Homepage**
- Hero section with search functionality (by location, genre, event type)
- Featured/top-rated bands carousel
- Genre categories (Jazz, Rock, Acoustic, DJ, etc.)
- Testimonials section
- Call-to-action for bands to join

**Band Discovery Page**
- Grid of band cards with photo, name, genre, rating, and starting rate
- Filters: genre, location, price range, availability
- Search bar with autocomplete

**Individual Band Profile (Public)**
- Cover photo and band logo/photo
- Bio and description
- Video gallery (embedded YouTube/Vimeo performances)
- Rate card showing pricing for different event types
- Reviews and ratings from past clients
- Upcoming public events with external ticket links
- "Request Quote" button (sends inquiry)
